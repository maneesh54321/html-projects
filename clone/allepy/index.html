Welcome abode. Sun 'N' Mist Kerala brings on a fresh dawn of comfort and luxury in houseboat cruises through the picture-postcard backwaters of the God’s own country. We have a variety of houseboat fleet, comprising 1-bedroom, 2-bedroom, 3-bedroom and 4-bedroom deluxe and luxury houseboats that help you savour the natural charm of Alleppey (Alappuzha), Kumarakom and Quilon (Kollam). Kerala houseboats have the right package, irrespective of whatever be your requirements (honeymoon, family trip, re-union with friends ...) or whoever you want to take with you (family, friends, ... or an entire organization)

We have several special packages, ranging from Kerala houseboat packages for sightseeing to special honeymoon houseboat packages. Our starting point in Alleppey is Finishing point or Pallathuruthy and the boarding point in Kumarakom is Cheepungal, near Taj Hotel, Kumarakom. Come. Plunge into an awesome world. There is no better way than feeling the bosom of Kerala’s luscious nature than a peaceful and unobtrusive journey in our luxury houseboats.
